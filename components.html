What are Spell components

Spell Components are verbal, material, and somatic elements that are required to preform a spell in order to properly cast it. In plae of some materials a spell focus can be used instead.

Somatic (S)

A spellcaster may use forceful gesticulations or intricate gestures when casting spells. The caster must be able to move at least one hand freely while performing a spell that requires somatic movements.

Verbal (V)

Chanting mystic words is required for most spells. Spells aren't based on words; rather, they are made up of a particular combination of Sounds, with specific pitch and resonance.

Material (M)

It is necessary to have specific Objects to cast some spells, as indicated in parentheses in the component entry. Component pouches and spellcasting focuses can be used by characters

Focus

Special items, such as orbs, crystals, rods, especially constructed staffs, wand-like lengths of wood, are used as arcane foci to channel the power of arcane spells. This item can be used as a Spellcasting focus by sorcerers, warlocks, and wizards.
