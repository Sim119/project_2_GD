About Spellcasting

Catena is a web application to share notes and ideas, by colaborating with peers on various studies... More on those in the categories below;

Enchantment

Enchantment is the study of affecting ones mind and compulsions, through spellcasting, enchanment spells can also be cast on an object to change, improve, and or add to its purposes.

Necromancy

Necromancy is a class of magic inwhich the spellcaster has control over the bounds of life and death, by casting spells to manipulate those who have died or are close death.

Evocation

Evocation is about utalising the raw elements of magic to create effects such as lighting a fire, or shaping water, evocation spells are often used in combat.
